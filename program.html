Draft Schedule

09:00-09:10

Opening remarks. Goals, scope, and community norms for FuseFM.

09:10-10:30

Invited session I. Foundations, methods, and theory for model fusion.

10:30-11:00

Break. Informal discussion.

11:00-12:15

Contributed talks and spotlights. Selected short presentations from accepted submissions.

12:15-13:30

Lunch break.

13:30-14:50

Invited session II. Evaluation, systems, safety, and reliable reuse.

14:50-16:10

Poster and demo session. Accepted papers, benchmarks, tools, and systems demonstrations.

16:10-17:00

Panel. What should the model-fusion community standardize?

17:00-17:50

Breakout discussions. Methods, evaluation, safety, systems, governance, and provenance.

17:50-18:00

Closing. Community notes, follow-up actions, and website updates.

Expected Outputs

  • A public list of accepted non-archival workshop papers, subject to author preference.
  • A curated set of open problems in model fusion methods, evaluation, systems, and governance.
  • Community discussion notes on compatibility testing, reproducible fusion pipelines, provenance, and responsible reuse.
